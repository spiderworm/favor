FAVOR Coin

Owe someone a favor? Settle the debt with FAVOR!

Sometimes we just can't find the right way to say ”thank you“. Favor Coin is the new blockchain-backed way to settle debts! Simply send FAVOR to the wallet of your debtor and consider your debt repaid!

What is FAVOR?

FAVOR is an ERC20-compliant cryptocurrency built for one singular purpose: to provide you a new and novel way to express your gratitude.

How can I gain FAVOR?

The primary way to gain FAVOR is to receive it from somebody who has some. Do something nice for them and maybe you'll get FAVOR in return!

If you owe a FAVOR but do not have any, details are coming soon on other ways to obtain some to send somebody some (or build up a stash for yourself).

I've received FAVOR from someone. What now?

Congratulations! You are part of a small-but-formidable force of people who have received FAVOR! You must have done some good, you do-gooder, you!

Feel free to collect more FAVOR, or pay it forward to someone that has done right by you!
